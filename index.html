Seminar Objectives

Our goal is to align with national missions like Make in India and Digital India by promoting skill development and capacity building in the field of AI and Deep Learning.

  • Introduce core principles: CNNs, RNNs, GANs, Transformers, and advanced neural networks.
  • Explore domain-specific applications in healthcare, agriculture, NLP, and IoT.
  • Familiarize participants with TensorFlow, PyTorch, and Keras implementations.
  • Enhance research capabilities and expose participants to state-of-the-art trends.
  • Encourage interdisciplinary collaboration between institutions, research organizations, and industries.
